What is Launchpad Kerala?

Does it bother you to attend boring placement sessions and interview preparations? Do you have the skills to do a job but find it difficult to prove it to employers? Then Launchpad is for you!

Launchpad Kerala 2024 is a premier job fair that brings together talented individuals and innovative companies in the technical and engineering fields. This collaboration between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn aims to create meaningful connections that drive progress and innovation in Kerala's job market.

Get Started

How is it any different?

We have all attended interviews where they assess us based on our exam grades. Lauchpad is an entirely different job fair for students and recruiters alike.

Roadmap

The challenge consist of these steps, influenced by the gamification in the MuLearn community:

  1. Participant registration
  2. Company registration close
  3. Challenge start
  4. Online exam
  5. Consolidated rank list
  6. Option selection
  7. Confirmed mail to selected participants
  8. Interviews
